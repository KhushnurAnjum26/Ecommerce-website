FAQ'S Page

What is the return policy?

Our return policy lasts for 30 days. If 30 days have gone by since your purchase, unfortunately, we can't offer you a refund or exchange.

How do I track my order?

You can track your order status in the "My Orders" section in your account or through the tracking link sent in the confirmation email.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we offer international shipping to many countries. Shipping costs and delivery times may vary based on your location.

Do I get a refund for damaged products?

Yes, we provide refunds for products that are damaged during shipment.

Are there any regular customer benefits?

Yes, we provide a special discount card for our regular customers. Additionally, customers who purchase items worth $400 or more receive a coupon.
